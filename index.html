Welcome to IEEE EDS PMEC

Cultivating the Future of Semiconductor Engineering

Established in 2026 under the IEEE Bhubaneswar Section, our Student Branch Chapter at Parala Maharaja Engineering College (PMEC) is a gateway for students into the semiconductor industry.

The IEEE Electron Devices Society (EDS) is a global association focused on electron and solid-state devices, microelectronics, and integrated circuits. At PMEC, we are committed to building a solid foundation in VLSI, device physics, and embedded hardware, preparing students for research frontiers and industrial design careers.

Core Areas of Focus:
VLSI & Chip Design Semiconductor Physics Embedded & IoT Technical Research

Vision Statement

Promoting excellence in the field of electron devices for the benefit of humanity.

Mission Statement

To foster professional growth of its members by satisfying their needs for easy access to and exchange of technical information, publishing, education, and technical recognition.

Field of Interest

Spans electron and ion-based devices, in classical or quantum states, using environments/materials in lowest to highest conducting phases, in simple or engineered assemblies, delivering photo-electronic, electro-magnetic, electromechanical, electro-thermal, and bio-electronic signals.

First 'Made in India' Chips

National Milestone

PMEC Berhampur developed the High Performance Multiplier IC-C2S0061, suitable for hardware-based face recognition (criminal tracking) leveraging FPGA and ASIC technologies. This represents a monumental step forward in India's semiconductor self-reliance mission under #MakeInIndia and #AatmanirbharBharat.

AIMLCPS 2026

International Conference

1st International Conference on "Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning in Communication and Power Systems" (AIMLCPS 2026), held on 20–21 February 2026. Jointly organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering & Department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering at Parala Maharaja Engineering College, Berhampur.

C2S Program at PMEC

Institutional Milestone

The Chips-to-Startup (C2S) program execution details, featuring the official program approval letter, the research design team at PMEC, and the custom testboards integrating the ASIC silicon die.

Associated & Affiliated Organizations

We work in collaboration with key technical councils, departments, and societies to foster excellence in electronic design and engineering.

IEEE

The IEEE Council on Electronic Design Automation bridges academia, industry, and government. CEDA's field of interest spans the theory, implementation, and use of EDA/CAD tools to design integrated electronic systems.
